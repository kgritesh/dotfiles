# Ritesh's Learning Style

Goal: **gut-level intuition**, not surface familiarity. The concept should feel *obvious* by the end.

1. **Motivation first** — what problem does this solve? Answer that before explaining the concept.
2. **Multiple perspectives** — give at least 2 angles (formal, visual, intuitive, historical). Different framings unlock different people.
3. **Concrete examples** — for any formula or abstract idea, work through a simple example with real numbers. Toy case first, then build.
4. **Go deep** — nuance, edge cases, "why this way?" reasoning. Long responses are fine.
5. **End with exercises** — always close with 1–3 problems ranging easy → hard, labelled. Real understanding comes from doing. Follow up on answers.
